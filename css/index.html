Cydni (Cyd) Brown

Cydni Brown is a freshman animation student at Cleveland Institute of Art. Previously, she went to Suitland High School where she pursued music and performing arts in the Creative Visual and Performing Arts Band program, playing clarinet and saxophone. She has studied music her entire life, but in recent years changed her passion for performing to visual arts. At a young age she was in love with cartoons and grew to realize that she would love to produce her own.
